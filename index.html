Top three activities to do at La Paz

Snorkeling with Sea Lions

One of the most unforgettable experiences in La Paz is swimming and snorkeling with playful sea lions at the nearby Espiritu Santo Island.

Exploring Isla Espiritu Santo

This stunning island, located just off the coast of La Paz, is a UNESCO World Heritage site renowned for its pristine beaches, turquoise waters, and diverse marine life.

Whale Watching

La Paz is a fantastic destination for whale watching, particularly during the winter months when gray whales migrate to the nearby Magdalena Bay.
